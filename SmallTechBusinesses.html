Small and medium sized tech businesses in LAVA

How to compete

Just due to their size and access to resources small and medium sized campanies (SMCs) have challenges to compete against large international high tech players. But, as large players suffer from bureaucracy, isolation, lack of interest, huge interia etc. there are potentials and opportunities to SMBs to compete.

See Opportunities

Adapt Quickly and Seize Opportunities - With much leaner decision cycles, SMCs can adapt faster, follow trends and seize opportunities quickly. By realizing speed, SMCs have the means to outperform their large competitors.

Leverage your customer intimacy - SMBs typically have strong relationships with their customers, allowing them to offer personalized service that larger companies cannot match. A tailored, well-designed service offering and software application can help overcome the problem of scale.

Harvest profitable niches - Focusing on niche markets that larger companies may not be interested in can complement the business proposition. But, because niches are niches, finding ways to offer products and services with low initial investment is key to ensuring profitability and return on investment.

And grab them

Accelerate and boost efficiency by learning from best in class - For a small company using their resources efficient is even more important than for big ones. Here learning from best in class is a chance. Especially large high-tech companies are quite transparent what approaches they utilize. They publish their research results and do utilize massively open source software, which gives SMBs the chance to learn from state of art and to start from a much higher grounds.

Be able to collaborate with externals and service providers - From time to time SMBs face large opportunities, where typically they have not the inhouse capacity for. Being able to utilize external helps to bring home at least some of these, is a must for enabling growth. As SMBs operate in niches non textbook knowhow areas, bringing externals quickly up to speed is a real contribution to SMBs growth potentials.

Learn to use upcoming advances in software and chips early - Software patterns are growing smarter, chips are getting more powerful. As development efficiency is always a top priority for SMBs. Improvements from recent breakthroughs should be their friends.
